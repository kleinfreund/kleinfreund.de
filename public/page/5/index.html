What have I bought on Bandcamp recently? (1)

March 3, 2015
In the past couple of months I’ve bought as much music as never before. The reason is Bandcamp. They basically provide the most painless way of buying music online out there. While Bandcamp takes a 15% cut (plus something for payment processing) of the music sales made from their page the artists are payed directly. You usually can get a CD or vinyl while at the same time receiving the MP3’s or FLAC’s directly after paying.
