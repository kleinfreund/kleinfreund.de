Website: artrudloff.de

I built artrudloff.de for my parents in Summer 2012. It’s a showcase of their work.

The site is a self-coded theme for Wordpress. Last year I added inuitcss and a custom Teehan+Lax Navigation.

Andreas Rudloff does printed graphics, aquarelle and placards.

Bell designs, woodworks, murals and drawings are the work of Anke Rudloff.
